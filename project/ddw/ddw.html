Stretch and Release

A series of handbags developed through experimentation with 3D printing directly onto textile surfaces. By stretching the fabric during printing and releasing it afterward, tensions are revealed, forming a three-dimensional textile structure.

This ongoing exploration questions how garments can be designed with greater care by extending their life, value, and relationship to the body.
